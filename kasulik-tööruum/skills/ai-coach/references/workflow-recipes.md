## Marketing Workflows

### Recipe: Launch a New Product/Service
**Time: 2-3 hours across multiple sessions**
1. `/setup-context` ‚Äî Create your product marketing context (positioning, audience, competitors)
2. `competitive-analysis` ‚Äî Map the competitive landscape
3. `pricing-strategy` ‚Äî Define pricing and packaging
4. `campaign-plan` ‚Äî Plan the full launch (channels, calendar, budget)
5. `copywriting` ‚Äî Write landing page copy
6. `email-sequence` ‚Äî Build launch email flow (teaser ‚Üí announcement ‚Üí follow-up)
7. `brand-review` ‚Äî Final quality check against brand voice

### Recipe: Improve Website Conversions
**Time: 1-2 hours**
1. `seo-audit` ‚Äî Find technical issues hurting visibility
2. `page-cro` ‚Äî Analyze page for conversion problems
3. `copywriting` ‚Äî Rewrite weak sections (headline, CTA, value prop)
4. `marketing-psychology` ‚Äî Apply relevant mental models (anchoring, loss aversion, social proof)

### Recipe: Start Content Marketing
**Time: 1 hour setup, then recurring**
1. `product-marketing-context` ‚Äî Establish voice, audience, positioning
2. `marketing-ideas` ‚Äî Brainstorm which content types to pursue
3. `seo-audit` ‚Äî Identify keyword opportunities
4. `draft-content` ‚Äî Write first batch of blog posts / social posts
5. `copy-editing` ‚Äî Polish and refine
6. `brand-review` ‚Äî Check brand consistency

### Recipe: Build an Email List
**Time: 1-2 hours**
1. `marketing-ideas` ‚Äî Choose a lead magnet strategy
2. `copywriting` ‚Äî Write opt-in page copy
3. `page-cro` ‚Äî Optimize the signup flow
4. `email-sequence` ‚Äî Design the welcome sequence (5-7 emails)
