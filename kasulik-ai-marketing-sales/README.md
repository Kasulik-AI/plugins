# Kasulik Marketing & Sales

Curated marketing & sales skills for SMBs. This plugin combines copywriting, SEO, email sequences, pricing strategy, outreach, competitive intelligence, and brand voice enforcement—everything you need to accelerate marketing and sales workflows.

**Before using:** Run `/setup-context` to initialize the plugin environment.

## Marketing Core Skills

- **copywriting** — Write compelling product copy and marketing messages
- **copy-editing** — Edit and refine copy for clarity and impact
- **writing-clearly-and-concisely** — Master clear, direct communication
- **email-sequence** — Build and structure multi-message email campaigns
- **product-marketing-context** — Gather and leverage product positioning insights
- **marketing-psychology** — Apply psychological principles to marketing messages
- **marketing-ideas** — Generate creative marketing campaign ideas
- **page-cro** — Optimize landing pages for higher conversion rates
- **seo-audit** — Audit and improve SEO performance
- **pricing-strategy** — Research, test, and refine pricing models
- **paid-ads** — Strategize and optimize paid advertising campaigns

## Sales Skills

- **draft-outreach** — Create personalized cold outreach and prospecting messages
- **account-research** — Research and profile target accounts and prospects
- **call-prep** — Prepare for sales calls with prospect intelligence and talking points
- **competitive-intelligence** — Analyze competitors and build competitive win strategies

## Campaign & Brand Skills

- **campaign-plan** — Design end-to-end marketing campaigns
- **brand-review** — Audit brand consistency and positioning
- **draft-content** — Write diverse content types for marketing
- **brand-voice-enforcement** — Maintain consistent brand voice across channels
- **guideline-generation** — Create brand guidelines and style standards

## Commands

- **call-summary** — Summarize sales calls and extract action items
